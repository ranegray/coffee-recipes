Aeropress

coffee: 17 - 21 g

water: 260 g

steps
  1. Boil water, 201-205°F
  2. Place filter in cap, rinse, and attach to Aeropress.
  3. Weight out 17-21 grams of coffee and grind.
  4. Transfer coffee into Aeropress body and place on mug.
  5. Pour 260g water over ground in circular motion.
  6. Stir.
  7. Place plunger on top and press slowly.
  8. Enjoy!

Chemex 3-cup

coffee: 25 - 28 g

water: 390 g

steps
  1. Boil water, 201-205°F
  2. Fold filter, place in Chemex and rinse paper filter.
  3. Weigh out 25-28 grams of coffee and grind.
  4. Place Chemex on scale, add coffee grounds, and level the bed of grounds.
  5. Pour 100 grams of water over grounds in circular motion.
  6. Wait for water to drop and repeat step 5 and 6 until scale reads 390g.
  7. Coffee should drain in about 4 mins. Remove filter and enjoy!
